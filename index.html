About MCM

Mapúa excellence embedded into MCM's DNA

Malayan Colleges Mindanao (MCM), a branch encompassing Mapua University’s technological and academic excellence in Mindanao, has occupied a 3-hectare land area along Gen. Douglas MacArthur Highway, beside Matina Town Square in Matina, Davao City

MCM has built a community of competent and innovative leaders who possess attributes that make them globally competitive and locally in-demand, distinguishing itself from the rest.

Within MCM’s DNA is the academic excellence that Mapúa is known for, and we are committed to bringing about the same level of excellence into our community here in Mindanao.

Learn More

Why Choose MCM?

Pioneering the blended learning system in Davao City, Malayan Colleges Mindanao, A Mapúa School, has contributed in shaping the educational landscape fit for 21st century students.

MCM intends to continuously expand educational opportunities in the new normal as it redefines learning with its flexible learning system powered by the world’s leading and highly advanced learning management system.

Learn More

Effective Learning Spaces

By fusing innovative physical learning spaces and digital learning environments, MCM equips its students with real-time access to learning resources and an inspiring environment to study, learn, and collaborate with thier peers.

Mapua University Legacy

MCM dedicates to bring to Mindanao the world-class Mapúa education that produces graduates that are highly preferred by industries here and abroad.

Learner-Centered OBE

Through learner-centered, outcomes-based education (LC-OBE), MCM commits to ensuring that student learning is focused on attaining industry standards through clearly defined outcomes.
